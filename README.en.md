# Lumen

**A menu bar lyrics app for riding the music.**

A macOS app that shows the lyrics of the currently playing song in your menu bar, with an aurora at the edges of your screen that reacts to the beat — so you don't just listen, you ride.

[![Download](https://img.shields.io/badge/Download-Lumen%201.0.1-blueviolet?style=for-the-badge)](https://github.com/hikaru777/Lumen/releases/latest)

</div>

---

## Features

- **Lyrics display** — Automatically fetches and syncs lyrics for the song playing in Apple Music / Spotify
- **Aurora on the beat** — An aurora at your screen edges that reacts to the song's kick, onsets, and vocals
- **Manual sync** — No lyrics for a song? Paste them, tap the tempo, and build your own sync
- **Fully local** — No payments, no account
- **4 languages** — 日本語 / English / 简体中文 / 한국어 (follows your system language automatically)

## Requirements

- macOS 26.0 or later

## Installation

1. Download `Lumen-x.x.x.dmg` from the [latest release](https://github.com/hikaru777/Lumen/releases/latest)
2. Open the DMG and drag `Lumen.app` into `Applications`
3. On first launch, allow system audio reading and control of Spotify / Music
